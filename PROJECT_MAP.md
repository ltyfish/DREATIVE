# Project map

## Public 1.0 workflow

- `skill/dreative/SKILL.md` — product-first implementation and visual-review
  contract.
- `skill/dreative/PLAN.md` — three concise delivery approaches.
- `skill/dreative/references/CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md` — product-native concept
  synthesis and reference-independence method.
- `src/shared/deliveryProfiles.ts` — CLI representation of those approaches.
- `src/cli/index.ts` — brief, installation, preflight, catalogue, and
  finalization commands.
- `src/cli/finalize.ts` — deterministic build/test/docs finalization.
- `src/cli/docsCheck.ts` — guards the streamlined public contract.

## Progressive design knowledge

- `skill/dreative/skills/*.md` — compact specialty summaries.
- `skill/dreative/references/CREATIVE_EXECUTION.md` — advanced runtime
  selection and ownership.
- `skill/dreative/references/VISUAL_REFINEMENT.md` — screenshot, critique,
  correction, and recapture loop.
- `skill/dreative/references/ASSET_PIPELINES.md` — bounded Sharp, FFmpeg, and
  glTF production workflows.
- `skill/dreative/systems/runtime.js` and `systems/demo.html` — executable
  golden systems and their browser fixture.
- `skill/dreative/llms.txt` and `src/shared/creativeCatalog.ts` — searchable
  twelve-system registry.
- `src/shared/projectContext.ts` — minimal durable `.dreative/context.json`
  validation and initialization.

## Removed legacy surface

The visual editor/server, deterministic design engine, and unused v9 plan,
evidence, approval, audit, critic, verification, and migration implementation
were removed. See `LEGACY_SYSTEM_REMOVAL.md` for the rationale and retained
safeguards.
