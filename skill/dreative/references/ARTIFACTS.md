# Delivery artifacts

Dreative does not require a planning database or approval ledger.

Normal delivery uses the application's source, its normal build output, and
browser inspection. Optional screenshots or recordings may be kept when they
help explain a visual issue; they are not a substitute for judgment.

`dreative finalize` certifies only deterministic local facts: build, available
tests/typecheck/lint, and internal skill-document consistency. It intentionally
does not claim that a model-authored attestation proves visual quality.
