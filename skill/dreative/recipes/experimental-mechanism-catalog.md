# Experimental mechanism catalog

Use this catalog during Award and Experimental planning after original chapter
candidates have been considered. Bind selected recipes to canonical v8
`experimentalPeaks`, `prototypeContracts`, `mechanismFallbacks`, route roles,
assets and current-run evidence.

The machine-readable catalog is `src/shared/mechanisms.ts`. It covers:

- media fragmentation and reconstruction;
- scrubbed frame or video sequences;
- persistent spatial props and WebGL/DOM handoffs;
- spatial product constellations with touch, keyboard and list alternatives;
- cinematic camera, reveal, media-plane and match-cut handoffs;
- shader/material transformations;
- reassembled, spatial and variable experimental typography.

Every recipe records assets, runtime packages, actual authoring tools,
specialized-tool dependence, performance risk, mobile and reduced-motion
translation, fallback, prototype scope, substantive delivery, insufficiency and
verification states. Packages never stand in for authoring capability.
