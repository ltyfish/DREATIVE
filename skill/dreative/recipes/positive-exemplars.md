# Positive executable exemplars

These patterns show control flow, not styling. Replace selectors, assets,
segmentation, timing, camera, composition and narrative role with project logic.

## Image tornado into next section

Sample the source image into bounded fragments, record each destination cell,
and use one scoped GSAP timeline to spiral fragments before landing them as the
next section's media. Reduced motion cuts between an authored separated still
and the destination. Dispose geometry/material/texture and verify 0/25/50/75/100.

## Fragment and reconstruct

Prepare a project-specific slice/particle map, progressively separate real
source pixels, keep the subject recognizable at midpoint, then rebuild into a
new content role. A noise overlay over an unchanged image does not count.

## Responsive frame sequence

Generate desktop/mobile manifests and posters with Sharp or verified FFmpeg.
Load a bounded window around the requested index; clamp progress, tolerate a
missing frame by retaining the last decoded image, and release decoded assets.

## Persistent subject across four sections

Declare four section berths and one stage owner. Interpolate pose, crop,
lighting/material and narrative role, avoid text/control occlusion, and resolve
the subject into semantic DOM. Mobile uses a shorter four-state path.

## Lenis + GSAP velocity refraction

Create Lenis with no autonomous RAF, subscribe `ScrollTrigger.update`, add one
stable `lenis.raf(time * 1000)` callback to `gsap.ticker`, clamp velocity into
the media-plane material and settle at zero. Remove the same callback and
destroy Lenis. Verify anchors, nested/modal scroll and no duplicate loop.

## Typography becomes media

Keep semantic text in the DOM, measure glyph bounds, map glyph regions into
media geometry or masks, then hand the same identity into an editorial image.
Reduced motion renders the resolved hierarchy without per-glyph travel.

## GSAP Flip shared element

Within a scoped GSAP lifecycle, capture `Flip.getState`, move the project node
between semantic containers, call `Flip.from`, and restore focus/reading order.
Verify reverse/responsive geometry and Strict Mode cleanup.

## Drag constellation

Arrange distinct project images in a bounded field, map drag/inertia to depth
and selection, then land the selected item in a dossier. Provide keyboard list
navigation, touch snapping, cancellation and a conventional semantic list.

## Video texture into editorial DOM

Show a poster until decoded, upload supported footage as a texture only while
visible, treat time/mask/material as part of the scene, then match the final
frame into DOM content. Ship codec variants, mobile crop and reduced-motion still.

## Cinematic five-beat chapter

Author preparation, escalation, peak, rest and resolution across separated
regions. One structural handoff connects the peak to a quiet reading section;
the hero alone cannot satisfy the arc. Record the whole controlled progression.
