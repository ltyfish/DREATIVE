# Dreative Specialist Skill — Refined Craft

Selected refined coverage owns finish, typography, material and optical craft in named production files and current visual evidence.

## Contract

Follow `../references/SKILL_CONTRACT.md`. Dependencies: `ux`, `mobile`; add
`media` only when media is selected as a primary material. Refined controls
finish, material discipline, density, and craft. It does not choose page
architecture or cap separately approved motion, interaction, experimental,
cinematic, or immersive treatments.

Use `../references/REFLEX_FONTS.json` through DESIGN.md's font procedure. Judge
fonts by brand and content fit, character, language support, optical quality,
hierarchy, continuity with valuable existing design, and performance. Common
and serif faces are valid when earned; an uncommon name does not rescue bland
hierarchy.

## Principles

- Clean, minimal, calm, luxury, and professional describe surface discipline
  and density, not low ambition. Restraint applies to noise, not imagination.
- A calm surface can carry unusual composition, expressive choreography,
  experimental interaction, or a restrained cinematic layer.
- Whitespace must create tension, hierarchy, pacing, or focus. Unclaimed empty
  space is not luxury.
- Photography is optional. Without it, choose a deliberate emotional carrier:
  brand-derived geometry, procedural graphics, animated typography, operational
  data, logo fragments, material treatments, custom illustration, or kinetic
  composition.
- Do not default to split heroes, credibility strips, alternating image/text,
  statistic rows, feature-card grids, or standard closing CTAs. A familiar
  family is allowed only when the visual blueprint proves it is the best
  project-specific composition.
- Preserve strong existing typography, motion, and interaction as design equity;
  transform or surpass them rather than replacing them for novelty.

## Craft pass

Build one coherent type hierarchy, spacing rhythm, material/light model, color
logic, state language, and responsive translation. Test quiet sections for
purposeful contrast with peaks. Motion should express the selected concept;
entry fades alone do not become a motion language. Keep accessibility,
performance, focus, reduced motion, media sizing, and loading behavior at the
same quality as the visible finish.

Done means the result is calm when requested but still authored: its
composition, hierarchy, material identity, and interaction could not be reused
unchanged by another brand after swapping the logo.
