# Immersive

Use when several sections should feel like one continuous environment or
instrument.

Choose one continuity owner and let it evolve through at least two meaningful
handoffs. Continuity may come from shared geometry, media transformation,
material/light change, or content state—not merely a repeated badge or canvas.

Alternate peaks and rests, preserve direct task access, and give mobile a
bounded form. Release persistent elements before they overlap later content.

Reject a hero effect that disappears downstream and overlays that compete with
the page. Read only the selected continuity system in
`../systems/NATIVE_FOUNDATIONS.md`.
