# UX

Use for every page.

- Preserve the primary journey and working behavior.
- Make navigation, controls, forms, errors, loading, empty, and success states
  understandable without explanation.
- Use semantic elements, visible focus, sensible tab order, keyboard parity,
  and accurate labels.
- Do not invent working claims, destinations, prices, or data.
- Prefer direct task access over spectacle and scroll hijacking.

Verify the primary journey in the rendered app with mouse and keyboard. Check
broken links, form states, back/direct navigation, and console errors.
